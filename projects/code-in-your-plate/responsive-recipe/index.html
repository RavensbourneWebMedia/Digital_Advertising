Raw energy bars

10 minutes

From Raw. Vegan. Not gross.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cups shredded coconut
  • 10 medjool dates, soaked for 15 mins
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • pinch sea salt

Method

  1. In a food processor, grind the coconut into a fine meal.

  2. Add in the rest of the ingredients, and process again until a sticky dough is formed.

  3. Scoop the dough by heaping tablespoons and roll each bar into your hands.

  4. Coat bars in ​chia seeds, hemp seeds, cocoa powder, or cacao nibs​.

  5. Place them onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper​.

  6. Store the bars in the fridge or freezer
